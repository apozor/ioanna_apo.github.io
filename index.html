Our Memories

Our love story

After 10 amazing years together, Apostolos finally decided that I’m the one (it only took a decade, but who’s counting?) and proposed during our unforgettable trip to Iceland.
Now, we can’t wait to celebrate this special moment in Thessaloniki—the city where it all began. It’s the perfect place to start the next chapter of our story, surrounded by the people we love most. We would love to see you there!

RSVP

Please let us know if you can join us by the end of March. Your reply will help us make sure everything is ready for a fantastic celebration

Accomodation

Thessaloniki is a beautiful city with plenty to offer, and we’d love for you to stay as central as possible to make the most of your time here.
There are many great options on Airbnb and Booking.com, so you’ll have no trouble finding a place to stay.

Transportation

In Thessaloniki, getting around is easy—taxis and Ubers are affordable, and the city is incredibly walkable.
As for the subway… well, after 20 years in the making, it’s finally here! But don’t worry, you’ll still get around just fine.
The only challenge might be getting to the venue itself, but don’t fret—we’ll help connect our local guests with internationals to make transportation easier. If you need any assistance, just reach out!

Our honeymoon fund

We know many of you are traveling to celebrate with us, and your presence truly means the world, therefore it’s the best gift we could ask for.
If you’d like to give a gift, we’re raising funds for our dream honeymoon to Everest Base Camp, and let’s just say, reaching new heights doesn’t come cheap!
