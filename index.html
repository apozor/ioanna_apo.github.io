Our Memories

Our love story

After 10 amazing years together, Apostolos finally decided that Ioanna is the one (it only took a decade, but who’s counting?) and proposed during an unforgettable trip to Iceland.
Now, we can’t wait to celebrate this special moment in Thessaloniki—the city where it all began. It’s the perfect place to start the next chapter of our story, surrounded by the people we love the most. We would love to see you there!

Wedding Venue

The wedding venue will be in Ktima Christidi in Ano Peraia.
View on Google Maps. The ceremony is hopefully planned to be outdoors, taking full advantage of the beautiful surroundings. However, in case of unpredictable June weather, we’ll move everything indoors. You’ll likely need a car or a taxi to get there. When the time comes, we’ll do our best to arrange contacts with local drivers to make things easier. In the meantime, feel free to reach out if you need any assistance—we’re happy to help!

RSVP

Gondor calls for aid... and we need your RSVP to join us in celebration! Please let us know if you can join us by the end of February. Your reply will help us make sure everything is ready for a fantastic celebration.

Accomodation

Thessaloniki is a beautiful city with plenty to offer, and we’d love for you to stay as central as possible to make the most of your time here.
There are many great options on Airbnb and Booking.com, so you’ll have no trouble finding a place to stay.
